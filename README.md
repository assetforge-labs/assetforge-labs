# AssetForge Labs

**World's first Smart Market Analyzer for digital creators.**

Package your digital files, analyze your market, preview your Etsy listing, and score your SEO — all 100% free, browser-based, no uploads.

## Features
- 🧠 Smart Market Analyzer — instant market intelligence
- 👁️ Live Marketplace Preview — Etsy & Gumroad preview
- 📊 Listing Health Score — SEO scoring like Grammarly for Etsy
- 📦 Smart ZIP Packaging — auto-organised folder structure
- ✨ SEO Metadata Generator — 13 platform templates
- 🔒 100% Browser Private — files never leave your device
